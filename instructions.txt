HOW TO PLAY UNO:

- The game is fully text based, and will give you prompts of which number will select each card
- You play with 3 npcs, who make simple random choices depending on the state of the game

RULES:
- You can play any card of the same colour on each other
- You can play any card of the same value on each other
- A skip will skip the next players turn
- A reverse will reverse the direction of play
- You can play a draw 2 on another draw 2 to stack the cards drawn
- You can play a wild draw 4 on another wild draw 4 to stack the cards drawn
- You cannot play a draw 2 on a wild draw 4 or vice versa
